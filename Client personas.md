# Client Personas — Bob's Mutual Funds Demo

## Alex Johnson (demo-client-001)

Alex Johnson is a 38-year-old male marketing manager living in Austin, TX, earning approximately $95,000 per year. He has been a Bob's Mutual Funds customer for several years and holds a mix of a Roth IRA and a joint taxable account with his spouse, with a combined portfolio value of roughly $241,570. Alex is in the wealth-accumulation stage of life — his primary goal is growing retirement savings while maintaining some liquidity for near-term goals like a home renovation. He is comfortable with technology and generally self-sufficient, but occasionally calls in when something looks confusing on his statement or when he wants a second opinion before making a fund switch.

## Maria Chen (demo-client-002)

Maria Chen is a 67-year-old female retired public school teacher living in Scottsdale, AZ, receiving a pension plus Social Security and drawing from her Traditional IRA. Her portfolio totals approximately $890,000 spread across a Traditional IRA, a Roth IRA, and a taxable account. Maria's primary concerns are managing Required Minimum Distributions (RMDs), minimizing taxes in retirement, and ensuring her beneficiary designations are current for her two adult children. She is cautious with technology and prefers to speak with a live agent rather than self-serve; she values patient, jargon-free explanations and consistency with the same representative.

## Jordan Williams (demo-client-003)

Jordan Williams is a 26-year-old non-binary registered nurse living in Chicago, IL, earning approximately $68,000 per year. They opened their first investment account — a Roth IRA — just 18 months ago and are still learning the basics of investing. Their total portfolio is modest at around $23,300, consisting of the Roth IRA and a small individual taxable account they started after a bonus. Jordan's goal is to build long-term wealth and get into the habit of consistent investing through an automated plan. They have lots of questions about contribution limits, fund selection, and whether they are "doing it right," and they appreciate encouragement alongside factual guidance.

## Robert Martinez (demo-client-004)

Robert Martinez is a 51-year-old male small business owner (plumbing contractor) based in Denver, CO, with variable annual income averaging around $180,000. He holds a SEP-IRA with a current balance of approximately $445,000, plus an individual taxable account. Robert is focused on maximizing tax-deferred contributions each year, understanding how his SEP-IRA contributions interact with his business income, and beginning to think about a succession or retirement timeline in the next 10–15 years. He is direct, time-pressed, and values concise answers; he has little patience for being transferred multiple times and expects the agent to already have his account context when the call begins.
